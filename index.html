Nutrition by Phase

PhaseTargetProtein

Fat ~25–30% of calories, rest carbs — higher carbs on training days. Adjust ±100–150 kcal every 2–3 weeks based on actual weigh-ins.

Today's Meals

Baseline is ~2,000 kcal / ~185g protein (Phase 1). Scale portions up for later phases' higher targets. Seafood-free rotation — swap in sushi occasionally if you're craving it (~500–600 kcal; add edamame/extra sashimi to keep protein up).

Sample Week (Mon–Sun)

Food List

Protein: chicken breast/thighs, lean beef, turkey, pork tenderloin, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, whey protein (seafood minimal by preference — occasional sushi is a fine swap)

Carbs: rice, oats, potatoes, whole-grain bread, fruit — lean toward training days

Fats: olive oil, avocado, nuts, nut butter — keep portions controlled

Vegetables: largely unlimited — add volume/fiber cheaply

Supplements

Creatine — 5g/day, every day. Confirm with your surgeon before starting.

Protein powder — 1–2 scoops/day as needed to hit protein target.

Hold off on any new supplement in the week before surgery (7/17) without surgeon sign-off.

Hydration & Extras

Target roughly half your bodyweight in ounces of water daily (~95–100 oz), more on training/running days.

Keep alcohol minimal — it adds calories without nutrition and blunts recovery, which matters more than usual post-surgery.

Meal Prep (Busy Weeks)

Batch-cook twice a week, then assemble by the numbers instead of cooking fresh every meal.

Batch Cook (~45–60 min, e.g. Sun & Wed)

  • Protein: 2 lb chicken breast baked/grilled + 1.5 lb ground turkey or beef + a dozen hard-boiled eggs
  • Carbs: 4–6 cups rice or quinoa; roasted potatoes/sweet potatoes
  • Veg: a big tray roasted/steamed, or pre-washed bags
  • Portion at ~4–6 oz protein + 1 cup carb + 1 cup veg per container

Storage

Fridge 3–4 days; freeze extra portions individually; label with the date.

Travel-Day Fallbacks (no kitchen)

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt cup + protein shake + banana, or a protein bar + hard-boiled eggs from home
  • Lunch/Dinner: rotisserie chicken + microwave rice cup + bagged salad; burrito-bowl order (double protein, light cheese/sauce); sandwich shop protein bowl or double-meat sub
  • Snacks: protein bar, jerky, nuts, string cheese, fruit
  • Hotels: mini-fridge for yogurt/shakes, kettle for oatmeal, microwave for prepped containers. Flying — buy fresh on arrival instead of packing a cooler.

Quick Grocery List

Protein: chicken breast, ground turkey, lean beef, eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, whey protein

Carbs: rice, potatoes/sweet potatoes, oats, whole-wheat bread/tortillas

Produce: frozen mixed veg, bagged salad, bananas, berries, apples

Pantry: olive oil, peanut butter, almonds, basic spices
